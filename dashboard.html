How to read this page
the pod
The 50 most profitable Polymarket wallets in the selected time window. This page tracks what they hold — it does not predict anything.
pod gauge
Each green tick is one top wallet backing this outcome; each red tick (from the right) is one betting the other way. A split pod is a shrug, not a signal.
price
What one share of the outcome costs. 10¢ ≈ the market says 10% likely. If it happens, a share pays $1.
fresh / late
Fresh: the price is still at or below what the pod paid — you'd get their entry or better. Late: the price already moved past their entry; our own testing shows chasing late has graded worst.
split pod
Top wallets hold BOTH sides of this market. Consensus in name only.
thin book
Under $10k of liquidity — the listed price moves the moment real money touches it.
moving now
The pod entered this market, or its net wallet count changed, since the previous data refresh. New conviction — the part worth checking daily.
standing
Held for multiple checks with no change. Context, not news: these markets (World Cup outrights especially) sit at the top of raw rankings for weeks.
the banner
Our forward test grades every consensus pick after its market resolves. Until the whole confidence interval clears zero, the banner stays orange and this page is a research instrument, not betting advice.
Data: Polymarket public leaderboard + positions API, fetched locally by run_dashboard.py. This tool observes; it does not predict, and it is not financial advice. Track record shown is from our own forward test (backtest_latest.txt in the repo has the full readout).
